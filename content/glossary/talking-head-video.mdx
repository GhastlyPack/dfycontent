**Talking-head video** is the format where a single person speaks directly to the camera. It's the workhorse format of short-form social content in 2026 — TikToks, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn video posts, X video clips are almost all talking-head.

**Why it dominates short-form:**
- Highest watch-through rate across formats (face on camera = social signal = attention)
- Cheapest to produce (no B-roll, no editing complexity)
- Best fit for personality-driven niches (founders, coaches, consultants, creators)
- Algorithm-friendly across every major platform

**Why AI generation is well-matched:**
The format has predictable structure: one face, one voice, ~5-60 seconds, no scene changes. Modern AI models (lipsync, image generation, video generation) are at their strongest with these constraints. A scene cut every 4-5 seconds keeps each generated segment short enough to maintain quality.

At DFY Content, every produced video is talking-head. The 14-day onboarding pipeline is purpose-built for the format. We don't currently produce:

- Long-form video (over 60 seconds requires different pacing)
- Multi-person scenes (one clone per video)
- Hand-camera demonstrations (the face must be visible)
- Product reviews requiring physical objects
