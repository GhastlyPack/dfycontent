An **AI clone** is the combined face model and voice model of a specific real person, trained from source media (photos, video, audio) of that person. Once trained, the clone can produce new content — typically talking-head video — in the cloned person's likeness.

The term is sometimes used interchangeably with "AI avatar," but a clone is by definition a copy of a real, identified individual. Avatars can be entirely synthetic personas with no real-world counterpart.

In a [done-for-you content](/glossary/done-for-you-content) context, the AI clone is the central reusable asset: one client, one clone, indefinite future content output without needing the client to film again.

**Legitimate use of an AI clone requires explicit consent from the person being cloned.** Cloning a real person without their consent crosses into [deepfake](/glossary/deepfake) territory and is illegal in many jurisdictions.

**Components of a clone (at DFY Content):**
- Character reference sheet — ~20 generated images covering expressions, outfits, key details
- Voice clone — a trained voice ID on an ElevenLabs Pro account
- Closet — outfit / setting variations that can be applied per video
